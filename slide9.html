Our Strategy

  • Since most of our data sits in the warehouse, Google AgentSpace is recommended as the primary tech, an addl. advantage is integration with Google BI components for developing reports;
  • Microsoft Copilot Studio will be secondary, aligning with our overall cloud strategy;
  • If there are other solutions for specific needs or technology, those will be evaluated for "Fit-for-purpose".

Implementation & GCP

The focus is on developing custom, intelligent agents within Google Cloud Platform (GCP) to create a robust, scalable, and secure AI ecosystem.

  • Leverage existing GCP infrastructure and talent.
  • Build bespoke agents tailored to specific business logic.
  • Ensure data governance and security within the GCP environment.

BigQuery Data Warehouse

BigQuery acts as the central data warehouse, storing patient records and consent data in a secure, scalable way.

  • Enables real-time querying, analytics, and reporting on large volumes of healthcare data.
  • Seamless integration with AI/ML pipelines for predictive healthcare insights.
  • Ensures secure and scalable data management.
